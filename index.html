About

I'm a fourth-year undergraduate at IISER Kolkata pursuing a BS-MS dual degree in Physics with a minor in Computer Science.

My current project, supervised by Mr. Raghunath Ghara, focuses on the 21-cm signal from the Dark Ages — tracing the evolution of neutral hydrogen and baryon–dark matter interactions in the early Universe. I presented this work at DPS Day 2026, IISER Kolkata.

Previously I interned at TIFR Mumbai, reducing HFOSC spectroscopic data for M-dwarf stellar flare studies, and at IIT Bhilai, where I analysed Gaia DR3 data for ~5.59 million stars to probe the Milky Way's formation history.

Outside research I write space blogs for Infinity Cosmos, coordinate astronomy outreach at ScienceOverse, and contribute to citizen science on Zooniverse.
